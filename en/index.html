Amolo is a hacking group made up of close friends, united by the connections and experiences we have built over the years on the internet and in the hacker scene. We created this collective with the purpose of strengthening these friendships and fostering the exchange of knowledge, always guided by hacker ethics and a collaborative spirit.

Our group is dedicated to the production and dissemination of technical content in the classic papers format, inspired by the style of publications from the 90s and 2000s. We keep this tradition alive, mixing it with our own aesthetic: elements of cyber sigilism, tribal traits and a strong cyberpunk appeal. Amolo is a reflection of the fusion between the mystical, the old school and the offensive — an identity marked by sigils, symbols and a critical view of the digital world.

Our mission is to share free knowledge, offering quality materials for enthusiasts and experienced professionals in the area of ​​cybersecurity. We believe in the power of information as a tool for transformation, and that is why we distribute our content completely free.

Welcome to Amolo — where hacking meets art, friendship and freedom.
